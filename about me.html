Introduction

Christine Nicole Chlapecka born April 11, 2000 is an American singer, TikTok personality, and left-wing activist. She is best known as a key figure in "#BimboTok", a TikTok subculture centered on reclaiming the bimbo stereotype and aesthetic in a feminist, and leftist context.

Personal life

Chrissy lives in Los Angeles with her miniature dachshund Sugar. She entered the music world with her debut single "I`m So Hot", after she released her debut EP "Girlie Pop" on June 5, 2024 and she returned with her album "Christine" on November 21, 2025. On June 5, 2026, the sequel to "Christine" titled "Christine: High Voltage" is released.
