About Christine: High Voltage

Chrissi return on June 5, 2026, with a follow-up album titled "Christine: High Voltage". This album feature 7 old songs: Passionfruit, Apollo, Aria, Bitter with You, Brain, Christine, and Girl on the Moon, as well as 7 new songs: Andromeda, Purple Moon, Spite, Female From, Invented, Cassiopeia, High Voltage, For Forever, and Smoke.
